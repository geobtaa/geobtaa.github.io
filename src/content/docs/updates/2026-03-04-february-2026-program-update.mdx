      Donnelly, Head of GIS and Data Services at Brown University Library and
      coordinator of GeoData@SciLi. Donnelly is a specialist in U.S. census and
      government data and a contributor to the Data Rescue Project.


      Launched in February 2025, the Data Rescue Project is a coordinated effort among members of IASSIST, RDAP, and the Data Curation Network. It mobilizes volunteers to archive government datasets at risk of being altered or removed from public access. The work reflects a challenge central to geospatial library programs: ensuring that authoritative data remains findable, accessible, and usable over time. The BTAA-GIN has contributed by saving backup copies of geospatial data from several federal government data portals.
